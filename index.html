Differential equation of the string oscillation

∂2u/∂t2=a2⋅∂2u/∂x2+F(x,t)
a2=1, T=10sec, 0≤x≤1


  • F(x,t)=0,
    u(x,0)=0, ut(x,0)=0,
    u(0,t)=0, u(1,t)=0;


  • F(x,t)=-22.5π2cos(2π(0.5x-2t)),
    u(x,0)=1.5cos(πx+1), ut(x,0)=6πsin(πx+1),
    u(0,t)=1.5cos(1-4πt), u(1,t)=-1.5cos(1-4πt);


  • F(x,t)=900(t2-x2)sin(π/16+30xt),
    u(x,0)=sin(π/16), ut(x,0)=30x*cos(π/16),
    u(0,t)=sin(π/16), u(1,t)=sin(π/16+30t);


  • F(x,t)=sin(40t)(20-16000x(1-x)),
    u(x,0)=0, ut(x,0)=400x(1-x),
    u(0,t)=0, u(1,t)=0;
