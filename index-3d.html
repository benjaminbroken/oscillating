Differential equation of the membrane oscillation

∂2u/∂t2=a2*(∂2u/∂x2+∂2u/∂y2)+F(x,y,t)

  • F(x,y,t)=0,
    u(x,y,0)=0, ut(x,y,0)=0,
    u(0,y,t)=0, u(1,y,t)=0,
    u(x,0,t)=0, u(x,1,t)=0;
  • F(x,t)=0,
    u(x,0)=sin(x), ut(x,0)=cos(x),
    u(0,t)=sin(t), u(1,t)=sin(t+1);
