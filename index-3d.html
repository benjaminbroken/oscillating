Differential equation of the membrane oscillation

∂2u/∂t2=a2*(∂2u/∂x2+∂2u/∂y2)+F(x,y,t)
a2=1, T=10sec, 0≤x≤1, 0≤y≤1


  • F(x,y,t)=0,
    u(x,y,0)=0, ut(x,y,0)=0,
    u(0,y,t)=0, u(1,y,t)=0,
    u(x,0,t)=0, u(x,1,t)=0;


  • F(x,y,t)=0,
    u(x,y,0)=5sin(x)sin(2y), ut(x,y,0)=5sin(2x)sin(4y),
    u(0,y,t)=0, u(1,y,t)=2.5cos(√5t)sin(2y)+10sin(5t)sin(4y),
    u(x,0,t)=0, u(x,1,t)=2.7cos(√5t)sin(x)-0.75sin(5t)sin(3x);


  • F(x,y,t)=-600sin(40t)(800x2(1-x)2y2(1-y)2+(6x2-6x+1)y2(1-y)2+(6y2-y+1)x2(1-x)2),
    u(x,y,0)=0,
    ut(x,y,0)=12000x2(1-x)2y2(1-y)2
    u(x,y,0)=0, ut(x,y,0)=0,
    u(0,y,t)=0, u(1,y,t)=0,
    u(x,0,t)=0, u(x,1,t)=0;


  • F(x,y,t)=-t2(ex-cos(y))+ (2-(t-t2)2)(ex+cos(y)),
    u(x,y,0)=0, ut(x,y,0)=0,
    u(0,y,t)=(1+cos(y))t2, u(1,y,t)=(ex+cos(y))t2,
    u(x,0,t)=(1+ex)t2, u(x,1,t)=(0.84+ex)t2;
